Neelikkandi Moideen Sahib Memorial Government College, Kalpetta was established in 1981 to provide quality higher education to deserving students irrespective of caste, creed, or religion. As the first Government College in Wayanad district affiliated with the University of Calicut, the institution has been committed to academic excellence and social responsibility for more than four decades.

The college is situated on a beautiful 25-acre campus at Vellaramkunnu, offering a peaceful and eco-friendly environment for learning. It provides excellent academic facilities, experienced faculty members, modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and opportunities for sports, arts, NCC, NSS, and various co-curricular activities.

Our Vision

"Kindling the Minds of the Youth"

Our vision is to shape the students of rural areas into responsible, confident, and knowledgeable citizens. The college nurtures academic excellence, moral values, leadership qualities, and lifelong learning while preparing students to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality higher education to all deserving students.
  • Promote academic excellence, research, and innovation.
  • Encourage participation in sports, arts, NCC, NSS, and other co-curricular activities.
  • Create social and environmental awareness among students.
  • Develop responsible citizens with leadership qualities and ethical values.

Achievements

The college achieved a significant milestone by being reaccredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC. This recognition reflects the institution's commitment to providing quality education and making it a leading centre of higher education in Wayanad district.

Many students come from economically weaker families, and several are first-generation learners. Through dedicated guidance, academic support, and mentoring, the college helps students build confidence, develop skills, and achieve success in academics and extracurricular activities.

Our Commitment

The teachers, staff, and students work together with a clear vision and mission to build a campus that promotes academic excellence, innovation, discipline, and holistic development. The college continues to inspire young minds and empower students to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.
